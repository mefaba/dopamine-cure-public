# Dopamine Guard Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-06

Dopamine Guard is a local-first Chrome extension. It does not operate a backend service, does not require an account, and does not transmit your browsing configuration or timer activity to the developer.

## What the extension stores

- Configured domains you choose to manage
- Your default session length
- Active session timer state for those domains

This data is stored using Chrome extension storage on your device and, where Chrome sync is enabled, may sync through your own Chrome profile according to Chrome's normal storage behavior.

## What the extension does not collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history collection outside the domains you explicitly configure
- No analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No remote telemetry
- No sale or transfer of user data to third parties

## Permissions

- `storage` is used to save your settings and session state.
- `alarms` is used to end sessions at the selected time.
- `notifications` is used to show a local notification when a session ends.
- `tabs` is used to locate and redirect open tabs for a configured domain when a session expires.
- `scripting` and optional host permissions are used to inject the session prompt only on domains you explicitly add.

## Third parties

Dopamine Guard does not send data to third-party services.
